Isaac Clark: Protagonist of Dead Space

Isaac Clark is the main protagonist of a popular and widly known and loved horror game franchise called "Dead Space". While yes there are some spin offs like "Dead space: Extraction" and "Dead Space: Ignition", Dead Space(2008) was developed by EA Redwood Shores and published by Electronic arts, but the main developeing team was Visceral games, and Motive. Dead Space(2008) was the main focus of the franchise, causing the smaller spin offs to be slowly lost to time.. When the game was Released on October 13, 2008, it was released on Playstation 3, Xbox 360, and windows. It had obtained four awards from 2008 to 2009: "BAFTA Games award for Audio Achievement", "BAFTA Games for original score", "GameSpot for best atmosphere", and "GameSpot for best sound design" .

Dead Space 2 was released on December 21, 2010. it was relased on Xbox Cloud Gaming, Microsoft Windows, Xbox 360, and PlayStation 3. It was developed by Visceral games and composed by Jason Graves. Along with the designers being Matthias Worch, and Ben walker. Was then published be Electronic Arts. This game had also won a couple of awards: "BAFTA Games Awards for best audio acheivment" is one of them, it has also been given nominations quite a few times.

Dead space three was the third and final installment to the series, while also being the longest and most action packed. it was released feburary 5, 2013 onto Xbox Cloud Gaming, Microsoft Windows, Xbox 360, and PlayStation. the third game while still being good, is generaly seen as less than the first two games because the third game really isnt horror, its more action shooter. The first game Issac never talked, once. It was claustrophobic, dark, and earie. The second game was brighter and less claustrophobic, and Isaac started talking, there were many more cutscenes.
